Shenzhen Avic Communications Equipment Co., Ltd. is a company engaged in communications antenna development, production, sales and service of high-tech enterprises. Our company has geared into ISO9001: 2008 international quality system certification, and passed TS16949 system certification, the products have GPS navigation antenna, GPS / GSM, GPS / GLONASS / COMPASS integrated antenna, 2G / 3G / 4G mobile communication antennas and LTE antenna, security series antennas, microwave antenna, the radio antenna, digital TV antenna, TD-CDMA base station antenna, WLAN / WIMAX series, 2.4G, 3.5G, 4.9G, 5.8G spread antenna, indoor coverage antenna, and other antenna series over two hundred varieties. Since its establishment， our company has been producing and designing a variety of antenna for domestic and foreign big companies , 60 percent products exported around the world, its scientific and technological content, quality and production in the country are in a leading position.

Our company has 10 years of experience in production and marketing of antennas, there are more than 20 high-quality engineering team, till now has obtained 10 patents. According to market demand, timely development of new products, according to customer requirements of high-quality precision manufacturing special antenna. Our company has large production scale, a mold manufacturing plant, injection molding workshop, hardware accessories shop, the antenna assembly plant. Equipped with automatic production lines, automatic injection molding machines, automatic lathes, automatic computer stripping machine and other advanced production equipment and anechoic chambers HP / Agilent variety of vector network analyzer, ADVANTEST scalar network analyzers, spectrum analyzers, analog signal generator , signal source, tester, SUMMITEK intermodulation tester, standard antenna test field and other advanced detection equipment.

With its technical strength, reliable product quality, customer first service philosophy, after several years of development, we gradually won the trust of customers, it has been recognized by the industry. Currently, It has become the most potential for growth antenna manufacturer in domestic and Asian region.
